**Thanos Teaches Typing** is a Marvel-themed typing challenge built for [Ludum Dare 50](https://ludumdare.com/)

my brother Cam and i pair programmed this one in a weekend. he came up with the idea and brought the 3D modeling skills, and i mostly drove the programming and app building. i remember going a bit past the deadline to get the web demo working but it was worth it

the game brought me out of a game-development-freeze that had been lasting for a few years. game jams are still the best way i know to get better at building by actually finishing something, and i love any opportunity to make games with my brother
